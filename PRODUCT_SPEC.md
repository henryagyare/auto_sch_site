# PRODUCT_SPEC.md
## Action Academy School Website

## 1. Product Overview
Action Academy’s website is the official digital presence of the school. It is designed to inform prospective families, support current students and parents, showcase the school’s academic and extracurricular offerings, and provide essential resources in a professional, mobile-friendly, and easy-to-navigate format.

The website should reflect trust, excellence, community, and accessibility.

## 2. Primary Users
- Prospective parents and students
- Current parents
- Current students
- Teachers and staff
- School administrators
- Alumni and community members

## 3. Product Goals
- Present Action Academy as a credible, welcoming, and high-quality K-12 school
- Help prospective families quickly understand the school and how to apply
- Give current families access to important school information and updates
- Showcase academics, student life, and school achievements
- Provide an organized, searchable, mobile-friendly web experience

## 4. MVP Scope

### 4.1 Core Public Pages
#### Homepage
The homepage should serve as a high-impact landing page and first impression of the school.

It must include:
- hero slideshow with school images/videos
- school mission statement
- highlights or quick introduction
- latest news and upcoming events
- quick links to key pages such as Admissions, Calendar, Academics, and Contact

#### About Us
Must include:
- mission and vision
- school history
- leadership / board information

#### Admissions
Must include:
- application process
- tuition and fees
- admissions-related FAQs
- call-to-action to schedule a tour or make an inquiry

#### Contact Us
Must include:
- school phone numbers
- official email addresses
- school location/address
- general inquiry form

### 4.2 Academics and Student Life
#### Academics
Must include:
- curriculum overview by level:
  - nursery
  - kindergarten
  - elementary
  - middle school / junior high
  - senior high
- department or division pages:
  - lower primary (grades 1–3)
  - upper primary (grades 4–6)
  - junior high (grades 7–9)
  - senior high (grades 10–12)
- library / media center overview

#### Extracurriculars
Must include:
- clubs and societies
- sports
- student enrichment opportunities

#### Student Support
Must include:
- counseling information
- special education / learning support information
- tutoring or academic support resources

### 4.3 Parent and Community Information
#### News, Events, and Calendar
Must include:
- school news updates
- upcoming events
- public calendar view

#### Parent Resources
For MVP, this will be a resource hub rather than a full portal.

Must include:
- uniform policy
- school menu
- calendar access
- downloadable forms or handbooks
- important parent links and information

#### Alumni Hub
Optional for MVP. If included, it may begin as a simple informational page.

### 4.4 Administrative and Compliance Pages
#### Staff Directory
Must include:
- teacher/staff names
- roles
- contact information where appropriate

#### Employment / Careers
Must include:
- current openings
- basic application instructions

#### Policies and Safeguarding
Must include:
- privacy policy
- anti-bullying policy
- safeguarding / child protection information
- other essential school policies as needed

## 5. Out of Scope for MVP
The following are not included in the first version:
- online fee payments
- full student information system
- gradebook
- live chat
- advanced parent dashboard features
- student performance tracking
- attendance monitoring
- internal messaging system

## 6. Functional Requirements
- Visitors can browse all public pages without authentication
- Visitors can submit contact or admissions inquiry forms
- Visitors can view school news and upcoming events
- Visitors can search for content on the site
- Admin users should be able to update content such as news, events, and page text

## 7. Non-Functional Requirements
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Fast page load times
- Clear and simple navigation
- Accessibility-conscious UI
- SEO-friendly page structure
- Professional and modern visual design
- Easy content management for school staff/admins

## 8. UX Priorities
- Mobile-first experience
- Prominent search bar
- Clear calls to action on major pages
- Consistent navigation and page layout
- Trust-building visuals and messaging

## 9. Future Phase Ideas
- Full parent portal
- student dashboard
- alumni engagement features
- online payments
- application tracking
- teacher login and internal tools
